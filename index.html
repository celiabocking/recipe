Blueberry Cake


Serves 8-10. Prep: 10mins. Cook: 50mins.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp butter
  • 400g frozen blueberries
  • 335g flour, plus a spoonful for berries
  • 275g caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 165ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest
  • 65ml lemon juice
  • 250ml thick natural yoghurt
  • 4 tsp baking powder

Methods

Step One

Preheat the oven to 200째C | 180째C fan | gas 6. Butter a 22cm cake tin and line the bottom with baking parchment. Put aside one third of the frozen blueberries and sprinkle the remaining two thirds with flour. Set aside both portions.

Step Two

In a large bow, beat the eggs and sugar until smooth. Add vegetable oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, and yoghurt. Combine with flour, baking powder and a pinch of salt using a whisk. A few remaining lumps are okay. Add the blueberries that have been mixed with flour.

Step Three

Pour the mixture into the prepared tin. Scatter the remaining berries on top. Place the tin in the oven and reduce the temperature to 180째C | 160째C fan | gas 4. Bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Remove from oven and set aside to cool. Remove the cake from the tin and sprinkle with icing sugar to decorate.
